About us

What is droplet?

droplet is an innovative weather web application currently in development by a passionate team of students dedicated to enhancing how we interact with weather data. Our mission is to provide users with accurate, real-time weather information in a user-friendly format, empowering them to make informed decisions about their daily activities.

droplet is the brainchild of a diverse group of students from various disciplines, including Computer Science, Animation, and Design. United by our shared enthusiasm for technology and the environment, we combine our unique skills to create a comprehensive weather solution that meets the needs of users everywhere.

This Weather App, droplet, is designed solely for educational purposes. We acknowledge that we do not hold the rights to any of the images referenced within the application. This project serves as a portfolio piece and is not intended for commercial gain.

The droplet webapp is an application created by Joshua Atendido Bear, Dan Finley, and Terry Vinson Jr.
